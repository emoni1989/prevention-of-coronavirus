Ways to protects yourself

Washing of hands always

Washing of hands always can prevent one from contacting the virus. Because most cases we humans we don`t know where we might have place our hands. so it is advisable to wash the hands always with clean water and soap.

Putting on nose mask

putting on nose mask is also one of the best way one can prevent him or her self from contacting the coronavirus, because one can be having a conversation with an infected person without knowning or an infected person might walk pass and sneeze or cough and one the droplet touches ones mouth or nose one can be infected. so it is advisable for one to put on a nose mask always.

Wearing of face shield

Wearing of face shield is very important,because it`s also prevent one from contacting the virus. wearing of face shield is really advisable because the face shield covers all part of the face.
